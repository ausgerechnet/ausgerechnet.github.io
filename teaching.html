Teaching

I am currently conducting courses for the computational linguistics program at FAU. Teaching mostly takes place in German. I also used to give various tutorials at the Department of Mathematics and the School of Business and Economics and lots of private tuition in statistics (mostly for students of economics and business administration). In 2017, I was a Google Summer of Code mentor on behalf of the International Distributed Little Red Hen Lab.
